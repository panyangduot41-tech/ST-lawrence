Level 1

🌱 Nursery School

Our Nursery Department is a warm, child-centred environment where young learners aged 2 to 5 take their very first steps in formal education. We believe these early years are the most important, and we invest deeply in making them joyful, safe, and stimulating.

Baby Class

Ages 2–3 · Introduction to structured learning and social interaction.

Middle Class

Ages 3–4 · Building numeracy, literacy, and communication skills.

Top Class

Ages 4–5 · School readiness and preparation for Primary 1.

🎨 Activities

Art, storytelling, music, games, and outdoor learning.

Level 2

📘 Primary School

Our Primary School covers classes P1 through P8 following the national curriculum of South Sudan. Students receive solid grounding in core subjects delivered by trained, experienced teachers who are passionate about learning outcomes.

Core Subjects Offered

English Language Mathematics Science Social Studies Arabic Religious Education Arts & Crafts Physical Education
  • ✓ Regular assessments and progress reports for parents
  • ✓ Homework support and remedial classes for struggling learners
  • ✓ Termly examinations aligned to national standards
  • ✓ Small class sizes for personalised attention

Level 3

🎓 Secondary School

Secondary classes S1 to S4 represent the academic pinnacle of St. Lawrence Academy. Our secondary programme is designed to equip students with the critical thinking, subject knowledge, and exam technique they need to excel in national examinations and secure university placements.

Subject Streams

🔬 Sciences

Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics

📖 Humanities

History, Geography, Literature, Economics

🌍 Languages

English, Arabic, Kiswahili

🧮 ICT & Business

Computer Studies, Commerce, Accounts

  • ✓ Dedicated S4 exam preparation classes
  • ✓ Past paper review and examination techniques
  • ✓ Subject specialist teachers in every department
  • ✓ University guidance and career counselling
