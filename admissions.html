How to Apply

The Application Process

Joining St. Lawrence Academy is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to secure your child's place at our school.

1

Visit or Contact the School

Visit our campus in Hai Referendum or call our admissions office to express your interest and receive an information pack.

2

Collect & Complete the Form

Obtain an application form from the school office or download it below. Complete all sections clearly and honestly.

3

Submit Required Documents

Attach all required documents (listed opposite) and submit to the school office in person or by email.

4

Assessment (Secondary Applicants)

Secondary school applicants will be invited for a short academic assessment and informal interview with a teacher.

5

Receive Offer & Pay Fees

Successful applicants receive a formal offer letter. Confirm your place by paying the registration and first term fees.

FAQ

Common Questions

St. Lawrence Academy follows three terms per academic year in line with the South Sudan education calendar. Each term is followed by a holiday break. The exact dates are communicated to parents at the beginning of each year and are also posted on the school notice board.
Yes, we consider mid-term and mid-year applications subject to space availability. However, we encourage families to apply at the start of term to allow for a smooth transition. Please contact the admissions office to discuss your specific situation.
All St. Lawrence Academy students are required to wear the official school uniform, which is available from the school office. Wearing the uniform promotes equality, school pride, and discipline. Details about the uniform are shared with parents during the enrolment process.
School fees are paid on a termly basis. Payment can be made at the school accounts office in cash or via mobile money transfer. A receipt is issued for every payment. Parents experiencing financial difficulties are encouraged to speak with the school administration early in the term.
School transport arrangements can be discussed with the school administration. Many families in Hai Referendum and nearby areas organise their own transport or carpooling. Please contact the school office to find out about any current transport arrangements.
St. Lawrence Academy is committed to making quality education accessible. Limited fee support may be available for exceptional students from low-income families. Please speak directly with the Head Teacher to enquire about any available arrangements.
