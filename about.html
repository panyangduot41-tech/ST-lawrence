About St. Lawrence Academy

A school born from a vision to transform education and lives in South Sudan.

Our Story

Education That Transforms Lives

St. Lawrence Academy was established with a singular mission: to provide world-class, affordable private education to the children of Hai Referendum and the wider Juba community. Located in the heart of one of Juba's most vibrant neighbourhoods, our school has grown to serve hundreds of students across Nursery, Primary, and Secondary levels.

We understand the unique challenges facing education in South Sudan, and we have responded by building a school environment characterised by structure, high expectations, caring teachers, and a deep commitment to every child's future.

At St. Lawrence Academy, we believe that when a child receives quality education, an entire family is uplifted — and when families are uplifted, communities and nations follow.

Mission, Vision & Promise

Our Mission
