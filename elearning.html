E-Learning & Digital Resources

Study support, revision materials, and online tools for St. Lawrence Academy students.

Learning Beyond the Classroom

Study Anytime, Anywhere

At St. Lawrence Academy, we recognise that great learning does not stop when the school bell rings. Our e-learning resources are designed to extend classroom learning, support revision, and help students prepare for examinations from the comfort of their homes.

Whether a student needs to revisit a Mathematics topic, practise English comprehension, or access past examination papers, our growing library of digital resources makes it possible — even with limited internet connectivity.

💡 For Students: Ask your class teacher for your login details to access the full digital library and past paper archive.

Digital Features

What's Available Online

Our e-learning platform provides a range of digital tools and resources for students at every level.

📚

Digital Library

Access textbooks, reading materials, and supplementary notes across all subjects and school levels in downloadable PDF format.

📝

Past Exam Papers

A growing archive of past national examination papers with marking guides to help students practise and build exam confidence.

🎥

Video Lessons

Curated educational video content for key topics in Mathematics, Science, English, and more — accessible on any smartphone.

📅

Class Timetables

Students and parents can view up-to-date class timetables, exam schedules, and important school calendar dates online.

💬

Teacher Messaging

Students can submit questions and receive guidance from their subject teachers through a simple, monitored messaging system.

📊

Progress Tracker

Parents can log in to view their child's attendance, assessment scores, and term progress reports in real time.
