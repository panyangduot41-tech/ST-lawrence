About the School

Rooted in Excellence,
Committed to South Sudan

St. Lawrence Academy is a private institution located in the Hai Referendum area of Juba, South Sudan. We are proud to serve our community by offering structured, disciplined, and aspirational education at the Nursery, Primary, and Secondary levels.

Our dedicated teachers, safe learning environment, and rigorous curriculum make us one of the most trusted schools in Juba. We believe every child in South Sudan deserves access to transformative, high-quality education.

Academics

Three Schools Under One Roof

From a child's very first steps in learning to preparation for national examinations — we cover every stage.

🌱 Nursery School

A warm, play-based environment for children aged 2–5. We focus on social, emotional, and early cognitive development through guided play, storytelling, and creative activities.

Learn More

📘 Primary School

Classes P1 to P8 following the national curriculum. Our trained teachers deliver English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and more with discipline and care.

Learn More

🎓 Secondary School

S1 to S4 offering a rigorous secondary curriculum leading to national examinations. Strong focus on STEM, Humanities, and examination preparation.
